Three things we got
obsessed with.

Most chat apps try to do a hundred things badly. We picked the three that actually matter for hanging out, and built them well.

Widgets, your way

Drop polls, music players, notes, watch-along players or anything from the marketplace right inside your channels. Each server feels like its own little world.

Encrypted DMs

Private messages and calls are end-to-end encrypted by default. Not even we can read them — and we like it that way. No keys to lose, no setup.

Make it yours

Themes, layouts, custom emoji, sound packs, channel skins. Express your group's vibe instead of fighting a default that wasn't made for you.
