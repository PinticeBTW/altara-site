02 — Privacy

DMs that actually private.

Every direct message and private call is end-to-end encrypted by default. Not as a setting you have to find — as the only way it works. We can't read them, our servers can't read them, the cops would need your phone.

  • Signal Protocol for messages, ZRTP for voice. Open source, audited.
  • Self-destructing messages — pick a timer between 5 seconds and 30 days.
  • Zero-knowledge backups — only your devices can decrypt history.

03 — Personalization

Make it look like your gang.

Every group has a vibe. Cottagecore book club, weeknight raid party, design crit — they shouldn't all look the same. Pick a theme, build one, swap fonts, change emoji, layer custom soundboards. Express something.

  • Theme builder — drag-and-drop colors, fonts, corner radius, density.
  • Per-server skins — each community looks like its own app.
  • Custom emoji & sound packs — animated, no Pro tier required.
